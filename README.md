# Threat-Informed Risk Register (TIRR)

A risk register that ties enterprise risks to adversary behavior
(MITRE ATT&CK) and mitigating controls (NIST 800-53), with a 
quantitative control coverage score per risk.

## Why this exists
Traditional risk registers describe risks in business terms but rarely
connect them to how attackers actually operate or which specific 
controls reduce them. This project closes that gap.

## Data model
Risk → ATT&CK Technique(s) → NIST 800-53 Control(s) → Coverage Score
